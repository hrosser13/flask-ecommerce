About Us

Our Mission

To provide an easy to use, eco-friendly service to students, helping them turn their unwanted books into money.


What is Student Swap-It?

Student Swap-It is an online marketplace for Cardiff University Students to sell and buy academic books! Books can get expensive and can be a hassle when moving out of university. Why not sell them on to another grateful student who doesn't want to spend hundreds on brand-new books? Win-win!


How can I sell my books?

All you need to do is register an account with us and post the books you want to sell - it's as simple as that! Alternatively, you can visit our location at Cardiff University and make use of our Drop-It! service. If you want to sell your books quickly and all at once, then you can pay us a visit and we'll buy your books from you! More information can be found here.


How can I purchase books?

We sell all of our books online on this website. You can buy books that students have listed themselves, or books that we have collected from students at the Drop-It! service and listed here. All you need to do is create an account and click Buy Now.
